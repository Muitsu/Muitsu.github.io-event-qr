Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 10, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the Event QR App (hereinafter referred to as "the App") handles information when you generate QR codes, save event data, or scan attendee tickets.

No Registration Required: The App does not require you to create an account, log in, or provide personal contact information to use its core features.

1. Data Collection

Because the App does not use user accounts, we do not collect your name, email, or phone number. We only process data that you voluntarily input into the app for your immediate use, such as:

  • Event Details: Information you manually type to create an event (e.g., Event Name, Date, Location).
  • Ticket/Attendee Data: Information entered to generate a specific QR code (e.g., Guest Name, Ticket Type).

2. Local Storage & Privacy

The App is designed to be fully local. This means:

  • No Cloud Database: We do not send your data to external servers. All generated QR codes and event lists are stored exclusively on your device's internal storage.
  • Validation Process: When an owner scans a QR code, the App checks the validity by reading the code and comparing it against the event list stored locally on that specific device.
  • Privacy by Design: Since there is no central server or user account, your data never leaves your device unless you choose to share the QR code image via a messaging app.

3. QR Code Sharing

The App allows you to share generated QR codes as images. This is done using your device's built-in sharing interface (e.g., iMessage, WhatsApp, Email). The App itself does not transmit this data; it simply hands the image file to your chosen messaging app.

4. App Permissions

To function correctly, the App may request access to the following:

  • Camera Access: Required solely to scan QR codes. The App does not record video or take photos.
  • Storage Access: Required to save your event lists and generated QR codes to your device so they persist after closing the App.

5. Data Management

Since there is no account to delete, you have full control over your data through your device:

  • Clearing Data: You can delete specific events or QR codes within the App interface at any time.
  • Uninstalling: Uninstalling the App will permanently remove all locally stored event data and history from your device. This data cannot be recovered once the App is deleted.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@example.com.
